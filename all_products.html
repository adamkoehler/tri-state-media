Thermal Transfer Labels & Tags

Thermal transfer printing – A printing technology from a label printer using a wax or wax resin ribbon that transfers the image using heat from the ribbon to a label material. Used for stock to extreme durability applications.

  • available in PolyMatte(TT), PET, Paper, and other synthetic label and tag stocks
  • available in roll and fanfold
  • requires thermal transfer ribbon for printing GO TO RIBBON
  • compatible thermal transfer printers include Zebra, Intermec, Datamax, Sato and more

Our selection of PolyMatte(TT) labels have a beefy 4 mil matte, white TT face stock combined with high initial tac adhesives for maximum printability and durability; laminated to a 40# calendared liner. TSM offers a variety of PolyMatte(TT) stock labels. Custom label, adhesive, and liner combinations can be made to order with any option in sizes and colors. In addition, core sizes range from 3 inch down to .75 if needed.

Laser Labels

Laser labels are made out of Pressure sensitive materials and contain an adhesive glue coated onto the face stock. The face stock will be printed on using toner and laminated to a liner. The liner is silicon-coated paper so that the adhesive on the face stock does not bond, allowing for easy separation.

  • available in vinyl, polyester, and paper
  • available in sheets
  • we stock a variety of laser labels. Contact Us for availability.

Our Laser Vinyl labels are known for their outdoor durability. They are resistant to cleaning solutions, alcohol, ammonia, water, and humidity. They have a quick-stick, aggressive adhesive and can stay adhered to fiber, steel, and other kinds of drums.
