Our History

Founded in 2001 by our President, John Clary, Tri-State Media is a manufacturer of labels, tags and print media specializing in the automotive, manufacturing, distribution, petro/chemical markets, and more.

When John began the business in 2001, he ran it out of his home with no employees other than himself. After 7 years of running the business, John brought his first employee on board in 2009. In 2010, they moved into a small storage complex and started with 1 printing press. Fall of 2012, the business was growing and expanding exponentially…with even more employees and tight space, Tri-State Media moved into our current location in Wilmington, OH. The 10,200 of space quickly became too small and in 2014, we broke ground on our new 10,500 square foot warehouse. Now, in 2017, Tri-State Media is a continually growing, thriving company. On average, we have grown about 15% per year over the previous year, and plan to continue doing so. We now have a total of 20,700 square feet of space that houses 25 employees and 4 printing presses!

Even though we have grown over the years, we still have a very small business mentality. At Tri-State Media, we greatly value the personal connections with our customers. We learn each customers’ individual process and listen to their needs in order to provide them with a unique, customized product at a competitive, unbeatable price. Our whole team works together to provide unparalleled customer service and great quality products.
