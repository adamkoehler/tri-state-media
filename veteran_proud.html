Veteranproud!

John Clary

John Clary, Tri-State Media’s president, is a proud disabled veteran of the United States Armed Forces. He was in Army ROTC during college and later served in the U.S. Navy. Tri-State Media is proud to be recognized as an official Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business by the Department of Veteran Affairs.

John would like to thank all of those who have served before and after him and those who have paid the ultimate price for the freedoms that America enjoys. Tri-State Media would like to list several websites to help those veterans become successful in their future endeavors. Thank you and may God Bless you.
